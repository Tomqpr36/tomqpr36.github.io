Testimonals

Emma Packenham

Multiple Functions 2019

Tom has played at a number of events for me, ranging from parties in my home and garden to more formal events in the Malmaison hotel. He really understands his audience and tailors his approach really well. Tom plays a wide range of music to appeal to all. He has a great rapport with the audience, and is such a warm and likeable character, making appearances at more informal events even more relaxed and enjoyable.
Dealing with Tom is a pleasure, he is informative, reliable and always performs brilliantly. My friends and family are equally impressed, which has led to Tom performing at my sister’s wedding reception and my Mum’s 65th birthday over the past few months. Tom is and will continue to be an integral part of my event planning.
